About Me

I’m a postdoctoral researcher in mathematics at the University of Queensland. I completed my PhD at the University of Maryland, College Park in 2025 under the supervision of Prof. Dmitry Dolgopyat and Prof. Adam Kanigowski. My main research area is dynamical systems, with a focus on statistical properties and limit theorems. I enjoy exploring challenging problems in mathematics and sharing ideas with the community.
